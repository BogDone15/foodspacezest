Payment methods

To pay for courses, you can use the following payment methods:

1. Bank cards

To make a payment, you can use Visa or Mastercard payment cards. When paying for the service, you will need to enter your bank card details in the payment form. The transfer of this information is carried out in compliance with all necessary security measures. The data will be redirected to the authorized server of the bank via a secure channel (SSL 3.0 protocol). This information is encrypted and stored only on a specialized server of the payment system. The site administration does not have access to this data.

2. Payment services

The company provides its customers with modern and convenient payment services to pay for services. For example, such as, Fondy, a payment service that provides a secure/encrypted internet connection and additional protection when sending payment messages. The service allows you to pay online for the purchase of goods and services from any country in the world.
