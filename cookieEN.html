Cookies Policy

This website uses cookies that are downloaded to customers' computers. Cookies collect information about the actions of visitors to our website - what products and services they browse or perform other actions. This is done in order to analyze the actions of website visitors and offer them the best deals.

The analysis of information from the website is carried out on an anonymous basis, as our company respects the privacy rights of website visitors and recognizes the importance of protecting their personal data.

When visiting our website, the Internet browser of each visitor transmits the following data to the server of our company: date and time of visit, browser type, language settings, type of operating system. We would like to point out that the IP anonymization function is activated on our website.

Our website uses two categories of cookies: (1) cookies that are necessary for technical purposes, without which the functionality of the site would be significantly reduced, and (2) optional cookies.
