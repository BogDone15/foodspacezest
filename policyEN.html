Privacy Policy

The Site Administration respects the rights of all visitors to the Site. We recognize the importance of personal information privacy of each visitor to our Site.

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Site and to the information collected by this website and through it. It does not extend to any other sites and does not apply to third party websites.

Collection of information.
When you visit the Site, we identify the domain name of your provider, the country (for example, “aol.com”) and the selected transitions from one page to another (traffic flow activity).

The information we receive on the Site can be used to make it easier for you to use the Site, including but not limited to:
— organization of the Site in the most user-friendly way — providing the opportunity to subscribe to the mailing list on special offers and topics, if you want to receive such notifications

The Site collects only personal information that you provide voluntarily when visiting or registering on the Site.

«Personal information» includes data that identifies you as a particular person, such as your name or email address. Whereas you can view the Site content without going through the registration procedure.

The site uses the technology «cookies» to create statistical reports. A «cookie» is a small amount of data, sent by a website, that your computer's browser stores on the hard drive. The «cookies» contain information that may be necessary for the Site, — to save your settings for viewing and collecting statistical information on the Site, i.e. which pages were visited, what was downloaded, the domain name of the Internet provider and the visitor’s country, as well as the addresses of third-party websites from which the transition to the Site was made, and so on. However, all this data is in no way associated with you as a person. «Cookies» do not record your email address or any personal information about you.

To view the material without «cookies», you can configure your browser so that it does not accept «cookies» or notifies you about their sending (we advise you to go to the «Help» section and find out how to change the machine settings for «cookies»).

Information sharing.
The Site Administration does not under any circumstances sell your personal data to third parties. We also do not disclose the personal information you provide, except as required by law (indicate the country where the server is located).

Disclaimer.

The Site Administration is not responsible for the actions of other websites. The process of collecting and transferring personal data when visiting these sites is regulated by the document «Protection of personal data» or similar, located on the sites of these companies.

The Site Administration respects the rights of the Site visitors. We unconditionally recognize the importance of privacy of visitors to our Site personal data. This page contains information about what data we receive and collect while you are using the Site.
