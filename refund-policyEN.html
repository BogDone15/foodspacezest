Return of Funds Policy

  1. The customer of the service (the client of the company) has the right to apply for a refund for the purchased Service before the Contractor begins to provide the Services.
  2. The Contractor guarantees a refund in the amount of 100% of the paid amount, subject to the following conditions:
  1. In case the training event is cancelled at the initiative of the Contractor.
  2. If the customer asked the Contractor to make a refund for paid services at least 5 working days before the start of the service delivery.
  3. The Contractor has the right to refuse a refund for the following reasons:
  4. If the Customer, for personal reasons, missed the services provision deadline and did not promptly inform the Contractor about this.
    • • In cases where the Contractor fully fulfilled its obligations and provided services, and the Customer received these services in full, but requires a refund due to the lack of personal results.
    • • The Contractor does not guarantee the result of the Customer after providing services, since the successful use of knowledge depends on many factors unknown to the Contractor.

    The Contractor does not return funds for the Services purchased by the Customer if the Customer has received unlimited access to viewing training materials.

  5. The funds are returned to the account of the Customer from which the payment was made. The customer pays the bank commission for the transfer of funds.
  6. Refund of funds to the client's account can take from 3 to 10 working days.

All applications for the return of funds are accepted to the Contractor’s e-mail.

  1. To send such a request, the Customer must fill out an application, print it, put his personal signature, scan or photograph and send the document by e-mail in PDF or JPEG format.
  2. An application for a refund must necessarily contain:
    • • the amount of payment for the Services, reasons for refund;
    • • details for the funds transfer;
    • • attachments, date, signature and decryption.
  3. The decision to return or not the funds is made by the Contractor within 3 working days from the moment the Contractor receives a written return application from the Customer.
